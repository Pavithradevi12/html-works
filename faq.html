FAQ
a. How do I use my coupon?

It’s as simple as snow! Addthe products to your ‘Bag’. Make sure that the products that you add are eligible for the offer. To find that out, select the product, and just below the price, you will be able to see the coupons that can be applied on that product. Once they’re all added to ‘Bag’, click on ‘Apply Coupon’ on the right hand top corner of the page.

d.What is the exchange policy of AJIO?

The refund will be processed within the defined timelines. If you have paid for your order online, the refund amount will be debited to the payment mode you used to make the payment. For cash on delivery orders, you'll have to provide the bank details during return creation.

e.Does Ajio offer end of season sales?

As a part of the Promotions, AJIO offers end of season sales from time to time (“EOS Sales”) which shall subsist only until stocks offered for EOS Sales last. The EOS Sales shall offer discounts on the MRP up to a certain percentage on select merchandise.

f. How do I purchase from Ajio?

Login to AJIO using your login credentials. If you are not Registered, Register Now! Product will be shipped to your Default Address. If you wish to change, do so before the offer begins. If your purchase is successful, you will be notified with a success message on the screen.

g.Can we change size in AJIO after placing order?

Orders once placed cannot be amended. However, you can partially/ fully cancel the order and re-order as per your requirement.

h.Can we cancel return request on Ajio?

If the payment is made via a bank or any other digital wallet, the refund will be processed within 5 to 7 working days. To cancel an order, log in to your Ajio account and go to my account section. Select the order you want to cancel and cancel it.
